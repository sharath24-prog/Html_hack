Internet Working?

The Internet is a global network of millions of computers and devices that are connected together.It allows people to share information ,communicate, and use services like websites ,email and video calls.

Important Terms

  • IP Address:A unique number given to each devices on a network.It helps computers find each other(like a house address)
  • MAC Address:A Permanent hardware address built into every network card,used to identify the device on the local network
  • LAN(Local Area Network):A small network inside a home,school,or office that connectsnearby devices.
  • Router:A device that connects your LAN to the Internet and directs data to the right place.
